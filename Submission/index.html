Invader Zim is a hero to the Irken Race

Invader Zim is a character from a cartoon series from Nickolodian released in the early 2000s

The cartoon was supposed to be for children to watch but gained a huge following among adults and has since become a huge source of memes

While the series didn't last long eventually a movie was made for the show called Enter the Florpus

Characters from the show

  • Invader Zim
  • Girr (zims loyal servant)
  • Dib (Zims arch nemesis)
  • Gaz (Dibs sister)
  • Professor Membrane
  • Almighty Tallest Purple (leader of Zims people)
  • Almighty Tallest Red (Other leader of Zims people)
  • Ms. Bitters
  • Sizz-Lor

List of Zims Mission Parameters

  1. Get to Earth without being seen
  2. Build a base of operations
  3. Intergrate himself into human society
  4. Commit acts of espionage
  5. Eradicate the human race
  6. Prepare the planet Earth for colonisation by the Irken race
